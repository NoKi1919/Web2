Welcome to the official page of the Liberal Union!

We are a party deeply rooted in the history of Borealia. Heirs of the Movement for Liberal Democracy, which fought for freedom and democracy in the early 1950s, we have always been here to defend the values we hold dear.

Our priorities for Borealia:

  • A strong and united Borealia: A federation where all cultures and languages can flourish.
  • A dynamic economy: More opportunities to succeed through the free market.
  • Responsibility first: Promoting hard work and self-reliance.
  • Simplifying citizens' lives: Less bureaucracy, more freedom.

On the international stage:

We advocate for a Borealia that looks to the West and its democratic allies while remaining strong in the face of international challenges.
